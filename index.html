Throughout this project, it is intended to determine the trajectory of stellar objects around a stationary black hole through solving the General Relativistic methods for stellar motion around a black hole using Euler’s method of solving Ordinary Differential Equations. After determining the trajectory it has been tried to simulate the motion of stellar objects around the black hole.

For this particular project, Python( a programming language) has been used and for simulation, a python module called ‘vpython’ has been used.
